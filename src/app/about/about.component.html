The team behind discovered




John Richardson

Co-Founder & Director

I started my career in recruitment back in the noughties, specialising in executive healthcare on an international level. With this niche specialism, Boolean has become a second language. I’ve spent many hours scouring the globe for the best talent… they’ll be no stone left unturned with me.

In early 2014 I decided to leave the rain behind and head for the sun, arriving in Dubai with a just a suitcase and Google Maps to find my way around. Living in Dubai, you realise how quickly it is evolving and how much technology drives this, along with Mark, we decided to combine our recruitment skills and passion for the digital world, from this we created Discovered in order to better serve the region with its Digital, Tech and Ecommerce plans.

As for my background, I have always been an IT geek at heart, from studying computer programming at college to keeping abreast with the latest trends and technologies of the digital world. A true lover of social media and coffee, give me caffeine and a meme and you’ll make my day.

Get in touch with me to whether you’re a candidate looking for your next role or a client wanting to discuss your new start-up or expansion plans.




Mark Carson

Co-Founder & Director

Entering my 9th year of the crazy world of recruitment and still love every minute of it. I started back in 2008, helping blue chip companies build world class IT infrastructure teams.

Then, in 2010, I saw a big gap in the market, Digital was the new kid on the block, I was hooked, I could see the potential and I made the transition from IT to Digital, supplying talent to the major Digital agencies, big retailers and financial services companies. Global expansion of these digital businesses came in 2012 Dubai was firmly on my radar, the majority of my time was spent helping clients relocate the best Digital, Tech and Ecommerce talent from the UK to Dubai.

In 2014, I personally made the jump, with never setting foot in Dubai before (some say crazy, I say just go for it). From scratch, I launched one of the UK’s best Digital Recruitment businesses into Dubai, establishing the team and supplying talent to the region’s leading Digital players.
