VIRTUAL PHASE

At the start of the 18-week bootcamp experience, students opt into a UX or UI focused path, then get exposed to the key skills and tools of that discipline. Because the material in the main program moves quickly, it's critical students be ready to start building interfaces right from the first day of the in-class session.

UX PATH

Intro to User Research, Usability Testing, Research Synthesis, Wireframing, Prototyping

UI PATH

Style Tiles, Visual Design, Interaction Design Principles, Branding, Typography & Color Theory, Prototyping

Details

  • 30 hours/week
  • Weekly check-ins
  • Virual coaching and feedback
  • Online peer collaboration
  • Tailored prep work

PROJECT PHASE

After mastering the basics, the Project Phase is all about applying what you've learned on both mock and live client projects. You'll be given the opportunity to work with a wide variety of outside companies to collaborate in teams and create real world deliverables. Check out the showcase for the kind of work you can expect to accomplish. You’ll also be assigned a mentor who you’ll build a close, one-to-one relationship with as you explore new career opportunities, develop your portfolio, and refine the work you’re doing throughout the program.

PORTFOLIO DEVELOPMENT

During this portion of the curriculum, you'll be given career workshops, studio tours, and one-on-one mentorship and guidance as you develop your resume, personal brand, and your portfolio. The goal of this phase is to develop case studies around the projects that you completed in the previous 10 weeks and to have a professional portfolio to begin your job hunt.
